About SnapJoy

SnapJoy CO., LIMITED is a passionate mobile game publisher dedicated to creating engaging and joyful gaming experiences. We specialize in casual games that bring fun and entertainment to players worldwide, while helping independent developers achieve their dreams.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talented game developers and global audiences, creating a thriving ecosystem where creativity meets commercial success.

Our Values

  • Collaborative partnership with developers
  • Quality-first approach to game publishing
